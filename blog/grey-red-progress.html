Development notes for the coming-soon album: placeholder art, machine tones, red horizon imagery, and synced picture experiments.

First transmission

Grey Red begins

Grey Red A red horizon. Black sand. A machine wakes beneath a torn military tarp. Old steel moves again. Triumph over the machines. The lock-on tone cuts through the wind. Something larger answers from the dust.

The first site pass gives Project Grey Red a coming-soon album room, a hidden machine synth, and a dedicated folder for future picture syncs. The current artwork is intentionally a placeholder: a grey-black field cut open by a red horizon while the album language keeps forming.

SoundFM machine presets are live for beacons, lock-ons, sword sweeps, reactor coughs, and servo twitch textures.
VisualsThe grey-red/ folder is ready for new web-ready picture exports when the Grey Red imagery arrives.
