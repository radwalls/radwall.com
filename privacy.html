Grey Red Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

Grey Red is an iPhone synthesizer created by Radwalls. This policy explains what information the app handles and why.

Information you choose to share

Grey Red lets you save presets only on your iPhone or submit them to the public Grey Red preset database. When you choose Save to Public Database, the app sends the preset name and synthesizer settings to our database provider, Supabase. Public presets can be viewed and loaded by other Grey Red users.

Public presets are not attached to a Grey Red account, email address, advertising identifier, or profile. Please do not include personal information in a public preset name.

Information kept on your device

Presets saved with Save on This iPhone remain in the app's local storage. They are not sent to the public database. WAV files are created only when you request an export and are shared only through the iOS destinations you choose.

What we do not collect

  • No account or login information
  • No precise location, contacts, photos, health data, or payment-card details
  • No advertising identifier, behavioral advertising, or cross-app tracking
  • No third-party analytics profile

Service providers

Supabase provides database hosting for public presets and processes the submitted preset data on our behalf. Apple processes App Store purchases under Apple's own privacy policy; Radwalls does not receive your full payment details.

Retention and deletion

Local presets can be deleted inside the app. Public presets may be retained so the shared library continues to work. To request removal of a public preset, email us with the preset name.

Children

Grey Red is not designed to collect personal information from children. Users should not place personal information in public preset names.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app or its services change. The current version will remain available at this URL with its effective date.

Contact

Questions or public-preset removal requests: Radwalls@gmail.com
