Opening image: a red horizon

Grey Red A red horizon. Black sand. A machine wakes beneath a torn military tarp. Old steel moves again. Triumph over the machines. The lock-on tone cuts through the wind. Something larger answers from the dust.

The first public checkpoint is about mood: desert reds, dead-black sand, old steel, and a machine voice that sounds damaged but alive. The synth page is the first instrument for that world.

Sound: FM lock-on tones, servo twitches, reactor coughs.
Visual: the smaller Grey Red gallery will reveal synced progress images as they arrive.
Story: machines returning from under cloth, dust, and signal noise.
